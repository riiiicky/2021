RAPP INTERNSHIP - SUMMER 2017

[ Overview ]

I encountered RAPP when I was researching on the Nestle Cafe campaign. I really resonate with RAPP’s design philosophy to build meaningful and high-value connections between brands and people. At the conceptualization level, RAPP enhances brand’s image. At the actualization level, RAPP makes the users engage more with the brand. I really want to learn how to build a better relationship between brands and people.

In summer 2017, I was worked as a creative intern in RAPP San Francisco office. Working with product manager, UX writer, web developer, and soft engineer. I got the training of time management by working with different clients at the same time.

Unfortunately, due to NDA I am unable to share all the projects, If you have more questions about my experience, feel free to shoot me a message!
