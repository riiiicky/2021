TRACES - ANIMATION

[ Thoughts ]

This is my thesis project during my undergrad. I am the director of this project, We experience an emotional reaction to our environment moment-by-moment: a like or a dislike, elation, joy, frustration. We ‘feel’ it. It’s personal. I’ve spent four years extending my understanding and process of design into the new territory. I like collaborating with different people, during my undergraduate, we've been playing multiple roles with different scales of groups. We learned how to communicate with other designers, how to tell a better story, and how to let people engaged with the animations. That methodology helps me a lot in the design field.
