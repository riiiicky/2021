OBJECTIVE

How might we engage blood donors and blood receivers?¬†How might we create a delightful experience for new blood donors?

  • The answers can be found in this Blood Donation experience redesign project. After substantial user research and observation, I added two fairly well-received features: Tracking & Personal Assistant.¬†
  • My first experience with blood donation took place during my freshman year in college. I saw this as an opportunity to help people.¬† When I arrived at the blood donation truck, I had to fill out many forms before I was able to give a sample of my blood. I was given milk and cookies after the procedure but did not receive any information regarding whom my blood might be helping.

DESIGN GOALS

    The overall goals were to:

  • Let people feel comfortable when they donate their blood
  • Improve the awareness about blood donation
  • Enlarge the donor community¬†
  • If I worked on the blood donor app team, bringing the app rating from the current 3.5/5 stars to 4/5 stars in the App store could be one of the measurable goals. Another measurement could be enlarging the community of blood donors.
